      Lean, jaw, taller, testosterone, skin — all come from 3 levers. Pull them daily.

      Portions + protein, not junk. Control rice, eat protein — the hunger dies.

      03

      Bodyweight keeps muscle, walking burns fat. Start tiny.

      Rules

      • Protein + veg first, rice last.
      • Water before every meal.
      • Cha: keep 1–2, zero sugar.
      • Nothing after dinner.
      • 1 cheat meal/week — planned.

      Adjust

      • Weigh weekly, same time.
      • Down 0.5–1 kg/wk? Don't change anything.
      • Stalled 2 wks? Cut 40g rice or add a walk.
      • Dizzy / flat? Eat 200 kcal more.
      • Hungry? Add veg, water, eggs.
      Not a crash diet. Never below 1500 kcal — it kills testosterone, growth and mood.

      05 · Mind

      Dopamine Rewire

      Reels, porn, nails = cheap dopamine on autopilot. Add friction, replace it. No shame.

      Reels

      • Watch one 10-min+ video daily to rebuild focus.
      • Log out, bury the app, 45-min timer.
      • Grayscale your phone.
      • Reels only after dinner, 20 min.
      • No phone in bed.

      Porn

      • Masturbation isn't harmful. "Nofap superpowers" is a myth.
      • The real issue = the compulsive loop.
      • Phone out of bed; blocker if you want.
      • Urge? 10-min timer, do something else.
      • Reduce — don't go zero overnight.

      Nails

      • Trim + file smooth.
      • Bitter anti-bite polish.
      • Give your hands a fidget.
      • Spot your trigger, guard it.
      Real dopamine: workouts, sun, study wins, real talk. Stack those.
      06 · Skin

      Clear & Even

      The tan + acne marks reverse. Goal: clear and even, not "fair".

      Morning

      1
      Wash — gentle, no scrub.
      2
      Redeent emulsion, thin layer.
      3
      Sunscreen SPF 65. Every morning. #1 thing.

      Night

      1
      Wash.
      2
      Kojic soap, 30–60s, 2–3 nights/wk only.
      3
      Redeent emulsion other nights.
      • Never pick or pop.
      • One new product at a time.
      • Less sugar = less acne.
      • Going harder? Adapalene (Differin), 2–3 nights/wk.
      • Sunscreen is mandatory with kojic/adapalene.
      Bad or cystic acne → see a dermatologist (cheap here).
      07 · Hair

      Healthy & Full

      Already good — just keep it healthy, don't damage it.

      Routine

      • Nizoral 2×/wk, leave 3–5 min.
      • Conditioner: ends only, not scalp.
      • Serum + oil 3–4 nights, wash out AM.
      • Rosemary tonic daily.
      • Hair pack 1×/wk.

      From inside

      • Protein (your diet covers it).
      • Iron, zinc, vit D — test checks ferritin.
      • Sleep.
      • Don't over-wash or use high heat.
      08 · Teeth

      White & Clean

      Mostly removing stains + tartar, not bleaching.

      Do

      • Brush 2×/day, 2 min, soft brush, fluoride.
      • Floss + scrape tongue.
      • Rinse after cha/cola.
      • Whitening toothpaste 3–4×/wk.
      • Dentist scaling — the real whitener.

      Never

      • No lemon, baking soda, charcoal — strips enamel.
      • Don't over-brush hard.
      09 · Height

      Maximize & Look Taller

      Maybe some growth left (late puberty). Protect it + look taller now.

      Protect

      • Sleep = growth hormone.
      • Protein, calcium, vit D.
      • See a doctor re: late puberty.

      Look taller

      • Posture — biggest instant win.
      • Get lean.
      • Stretch chest + hip flexors.
      • Fitted clothes, vertical lines.

      Never

      • No surgery.
      • No height pills / hormones.
      • Short ≠ over.
      10 · Labs

      Get Your Baseline

      Never had bloodwork. Cheap here. Ask your parents + a doctor for:

      • Glucose + HbA1c
      • Fasting insulin
      • Lipid profile
      • TSH + free T4
      • Testosterone, LH, FSH
      • Vitamin D
      • CBC
      • Ferritin
      • Liver (ALT/AST)
      A doctor reads these. Never self-medicate or buy hormones.
